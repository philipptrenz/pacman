Instructions

The game is inspired by the original arcade game. You control Pac-Man through a maze, eating pac-dots. When all pac-dots are eaten, Pac-Man is taken to the next level. Three ghosts roam the maze. If Pac-Man cames too close to them, they will persecute him. But although they are ghosts, they can't look through walls, so be fast and escape them! If a ghost touches Pac-Man, you lose one of three lifes and the level starts from beginning. When you lost the last life, you will get back to the first level.
